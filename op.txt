It occurs in the sub-Himalayan tract from 
the Indus eastwards to the Khasi Hills,West Bengal, 
Indian Peninsula and the Andamans. It is a component of 
Tropical Evergreen, Semi evergreen Moist and
Dry deciduous forests.


The management of the forests under
 selection and clear-felling silvicultural systems
 is practiced in a limited number of countries, 
mostly in Asia, although in recent years several 
countries have seriously taken up silviculture and the rehabilitation 
of degraded forest formations.
